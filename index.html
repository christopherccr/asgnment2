Topic Background

Traffic accident has always been a trending topic since very long ago. Based on the data collected from all over the world, traffic accident has given rise to tons of death cases. Thus, all of us must take this issue seriously to reduce the number of traffic accidents. There are a lot of factors causing a road accident to take place. This data visualization allows us to inspect and analyse these factors.

Conclusion

Based on the analysis of visualization above, the developed country and country with more populations tend to have more accident cases. Generally, road accident also happens more frequently during daytime. Lastly, the death rate for male is higher in road accident as compared to female. The traffic accident can be reduced by encouraging the citizens to take public transport and enforcing the traffic law.
